UV are place on cross like this:

      0   u1  u2  1
    0 +---+---+---+
      | . | T | . |
    v1+---+---+---+
      | L | F | R |
    v2+---+---+---+
      | . | B | . |
    v3+---+---+---+
      | . | b | . |
    1 +---+---+---+
Content copied to clipboard

Where:

  • T : Applied on top face

  • L : Applied on left face

  • F : Applied on face face

  • R : Applied on right face

  • B : Applied on bottom face

  • b : Applied on back face

  • Other part of texture are not used

Parameters

u1

U border between left and face

u2

U border between face and right

v1

V border between top and face

v2

V border between face and bottom

v3

V border between bottom and back
